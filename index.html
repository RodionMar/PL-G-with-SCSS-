About
PL & G
Peace Love & Guitars is a religious movement. It is about bringing the world together through live, original music. It is a series of live music clubs, where musicians can gather, collaborate, write and create. Peace Love & Guitars is an incubator for finding, launching, and managing future musicians. It is a record label that will produce new artists for an international market and a cool place to come hang out, have a few drinks, and listen to original live music.
Live Music Club
Modeled after the world’s great independent clubs, PG&L will feature original, live “open mic” music (4) nights a week.
Recording Studio
Featuring the Tascam Model 24 Recorder, PG&L will have a built-in recording studio available to use 24/7
Talent Incubator
PG&L will nuture and grow talented musicians. It’s a place where future stars can be amde.
Cafe/Soup kitchen
PG&L will sell food and drink, but will provide free meals to those who need a little extra help.
